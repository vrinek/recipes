Brownie/blondie marble Bundt cake

Ingredients

  • butter: 85g
  • vanilla extract: 5ml
  • brown sugar: 200g
  • eggs: 3
  • flour: 125g
  • salt: a pinch
  • butter: 85g
  • dark baking chocolate: 140g
  • vanilla extract: 5ml
  • brown sugar: 250g
  • eggs: 3
  • flour: 125g
  • unsweetened cocoa powder: 25g
  • chocolate chips: 100g

Cookware

  • Bundt cake molde

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 C
  2. Grease Bundt cake molde with butter, and flour (so it doesn't stick)
  3. Melt the butter (85g)
  4. Put the melted butter in a big bowl
  5. Add vanilla extract (5ml) and brown sugar (200g)
  6. Beat until fluffy (2-3min)
  7. Add eggs (3), one at a time, beating each time to get them mixed well
  8. Mix in flour (125g)
  9. Add salt (a pinch)
  10. Melt the butter (85g) and the dark baking chocolate (140g)
  11. Put the melted butter/chocolate mixture in a big bowl
  12. Add vanilla extract (5ml) and brown sugar (250g)
  13. Beat until fluffy (2-3min)
  14. Add eggs (3), one at a time, beating each time to get them mixed well
  15. Mix in flour (125g) and unsweetened cocoa powder (25g)
  16. Fold in chocolate chips (100g) with a spatula
  17. Pour the batters into the greased Bundt cake molde, alternating between brownie and blondie in small amounts
  18. Use a knife to gently swirl the batters together, creating a marble pattern
  19. Bake for 65 minutes
  20. To check if it's done, use a knife. It should come out with no batter. It's okay if some melted chocolate sticks to it.
